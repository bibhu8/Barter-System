How It Works

Learn how to start swapping and trading your items with others.

1. Create an Account

Sign up for a free account to start listing your items and browsing what others have to offer.

2. List Your Items

Take photos and create listings for items you'd like to trade. Add descriptions to help others understand what you're offering.

3. Browse & Find

Explore items that other users have listed. Use filters to find exactly what you're looking for.

4. Make an Offer

When you find something you like, make a swap offer with your own items. Message the owner to discuss details.

5. Complete the Trade

Once both parties agree, arrange to exchange items. After the swap, leave feedback about your experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Swap & Trade completely free?

Yes! Our basic service is completely free. We may offer premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always be free to use.

How do I ensure a safe trade?

We recommend meeting in public places for in-person trades, checking user ratings before agreeing to swap, and using our messaging system to keep all communications on the platform.

What items are not allowed on the platform?

Prohibited items include illegal goods, dangerous materials, counterfeit products, and adult content. Please review our full terms of service for details.
